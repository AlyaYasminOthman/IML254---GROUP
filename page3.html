Got Questions?

What are your hours of operation?

We are open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM Monday through Friday, and from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM on Saturday and Sunday. Please note that hours may vary on holidays.

Do you take reservation?

Unfortunately, we do not take reservations as all our desserts are freshly made daily. We encourage you to visit early to ensure availability.

What types of desserts do you offer?

Our menu features a variety of desserts including muffins, macarons, meringue, cookies, pudding, jelly, and drinks. We also have gluten-free and vegan options available

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a selection of vegan and gluten-free desserts. Please ask our staff for recommendations when you visit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and mobile payments such as Apple Pay, Google Pay, and DuitNow QR. All payment must be made in the store after the customer receive their order, except the delivery.

Are your desserts made fresh daily?

Yes, all our desserts are freshly made daily using high-quality ingredients to ensure the best taste and quality.
